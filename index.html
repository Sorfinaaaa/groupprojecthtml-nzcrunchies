About Our NZCrunchies

NZCrunchies's Snack

A manufacturer of food, NZ Frozen Industries Sdn Bhd focuses on creating frozen foods (NZ Food®), including pastries and snacks made from spring roll wrappers (NZCrunchies®). Since 1997, we have been in business and have earned the trust of clients in Malaysia, Singapore, Brunei, and Saudi Arabia.

Our NZCrunchies® snacks satisfy snack lovers all over the world with their distinctive flavours and thin, crispy texture. It's difficult to stop snacking while savouring the flavour since the sound of the snack crackling in your mouth is tempting.
